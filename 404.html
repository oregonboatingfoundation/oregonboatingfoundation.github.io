Programs

Our programs are designed to get you out on the water, and teach the skills you need to stay safe.

Guided Kayak Tours

Guided Kayak Tours

Learn about Newport's fishing fleet, landmarks, wildlife and natural history in these unique two-hour tours as we explore Yaquina Bay.

Learn More
Youth Camps

Youth Camps

Designed for youth between the ages of 10 and 18, our five-day youth sailing and kayaking day camps are affordable and fun.

Learn More
Sea Kayak Classes

Sea Kayak Classes

Our one-day sea kayak classes – primarily for adults – help build the skills you need to confidently enjoy kayaking on your own.

Learn More
Free Family Boating

Free Family Boating

Free weekly events – run by the Port of Toledo – where your whole family can try boating in a safe and supervised environment.
