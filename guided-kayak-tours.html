Newport Kayak Tours

Book Online

Experience the Historic Newport Bayfront

Our guided tours will start in June 2019

The perfect adventure for your family vacation at the Oregon Coast. Our guided kayak tours offer an incredible opportunity to get up close and personal with the wildlife, fishing community, and landmarks of Yaquina Bay and the Newport Bayfront. No experience is necessary. Available for ages 6 and up (children under 13 must be accompanied by an adult).

Our passionate, certified guides will introduce you to the fishing fleet that brings in more fresh seafood than any other port on the West Coast. Wildlife, including sea lions, harbor seals, eagles, and so much more, awaits you during this unique two-hour kayak trip that is perfect for singles, couples, groups, and families.

Tours run from Tuesday to Sunday during the summer – starting at 9am, 11am, 3pm, and 6pm. Whether you want to go for a quiet morning paddle or watch the sun set behind the history Yaquina Bay bridge, we'll help you craft an unforgettable trip.

Our tours launch from the OBF BoathouseOBF Boathouse located at Port Dock 7 in Newport, Oregon. Please plan on arriving 20 minutes before your scheduled tour to maximize your time on the water. We look forward to taking you on a memorable adventure during your next vacation to Newport and the Oregon Coast.

What To Bring & Wear

Our guided kayak tours run "rain or shine", so you'll want to be prepared for the conditions on the day of your tour. Your feet will definitely get a little wet. We recommend the following items:

  • Water bottle
  • Sunglasses and/or a hat
  • Sunscreen
  • Quick dry clothing (cotton is not recommended)
  • Extra layers (a windbreaker or rain jacket/pants)
  • Footwear that can get wet
  • Waterproof case for your smartphone and/or camera
  • Gratuity for your guide (optional, but appreciated!)

How To Find Us

Our tours launch from the OBF BoathouseOBF Boathouse located at Port Dock 7 in Newport, Oregon. Parking is available at the Port of Newport, with additional on-street parking on SE Bay Blvd. Please remember that Newport is a working port, so we ask that you be respectful and park closest to the street so that the fishermen who work at Port Dock 7 have priority access.

Once you have arrived, to get to the OBF Boathouse simply go down the access ramp to the docks, and take an immediate left at the bottom of the ramp (at the Chelsea Rose seafood barge). Walk past the sailboats and we're the first boathouse that you come upon.

Cancellation Policy

We require at least 24 hours cancellation notice prior to your scheduled tour, or we cannot provide a refund. Our tours require at least 3 participants. If your scheduled tour is canceled, we'll notify you at least 24 hours prior to the start of the tour.

All of our tours are weather dependent, and may be cancelled due to inclement weather or other unsafe conditions. If your scheduled tour is cancelled due to weather or safety concerns, you'll receive a full refund.
