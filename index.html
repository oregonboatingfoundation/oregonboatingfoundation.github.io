Get Out on the Water Safely!

The Oregon Boating Foundation is a nonprofit organization whose mission is to get you out on the water safely. We operate out of Newport – on the Oregon Coast – where we have access to some spectacular waterways.

Being on the water is a way of life in our community, and our goal is to ensure that safe, affordable boating opportunities are accessible to everyone:

  • Kayak & Sailing Youth Camps
  • Kayak Tours in Yaquina Bay
  • Sea Kayak Classes
  • Free Family Boating
