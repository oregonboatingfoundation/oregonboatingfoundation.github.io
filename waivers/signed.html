Programs

Our programs are designed to get you out on the water, and teach the skills you need to stay safe.

Youth Camps

Youth Camps

Designed for youth between the ages of 10 and 18, our five-day youth sailing and kayaking day camps are an affordable, fun way to spend Summer days.

Learn More
Sea Kayak Classes

Sea Kayak Classes

Our one-day sea kayak classes – designed primarily for adults – help build the skills to confidently enjoy kayaking on your own.

Learn More
Free Family Boating

Free Family Boating

Free weekly events – run by the Port of Toledo – where your whole family can give boating a try in a safe and supervised environment.
