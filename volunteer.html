Volunteer

Become A Volunteer

Volunteers are the lifeblood of our organization, and we welcome volunteers who are interested in sharing their experience and time with others.

Our volunteers serve as instructors, assistants, safety and even as just general helpers. If you are interested in volunteering your time, please start by familiarizing yourself with our organization and policies:

  • Volunteer Handbook
  • Youth Protection Policy
  • Volunteer Application

Then simply fill out an application or call us at 800-806-4882 to schedule an interview.

Programs

Our programs are purposefully designed to teach the skills needed to get out on the water safely.

Youth Camps

Youth Camps

Our five-day youth sailing and kayak camps are designed for ages 10 through 18, and provide an affordable, fun way to spend your Summer days.

Learn More
Sea Kayak Classes

Sea Kayak Classes

Our one-day kayak classes are designed primarily for adults, and help you build the skills to confidently enjoy kayaking on your own.

Learn More
Free Family Boating

Free Family Boating

These weekly events – run by the Port of Toledo – allow your whole family the opportunity to give boating a try in a safe, supervised environment.
