We Rely on Your Generosity

As a 501(c)(3) charitable organization, we rely on generous contributions from those interested in supporting our vision of making affordable boating education programs available to everyone on the Central Oregon Coast.

If you have specific instructions for your donation, or are interested in discussing planned giving strategies, please contact our Executive Director by calling (800) 806-4882 , or sending us an email.
