Make A Donation

Your generous donations help provide affordable boating opportunities in our community.

As a 501(c)(3) charitable organization, we rely on generous contributions from those interested in supporting our vision of making affordable boating education programs available to everyone on the Central Oregon Coast.

If you have specific instructions for your donation, or are interested in discussing planned giving strategies, please contact our Executive Director by calling (800) 806-4882 , or sending an email to director@oregonboatingfoundation.org.

How Your Generosity Helps

A $50 Donation…

Buys lunches, snacks, waters and other necessities young people need to fuel a day of having fun on the water.

Donate $50

A $150 Donation…

Pays the program fees for those facing financial challenges who may otherwise not be able to participate.

Donate $150

A $500 Donation…

Pays for life jackets, sails, paddles and other equipment needed to ensure that we can provide safe experiences.

Donate $500

A $1,000 Donation…

Pays for the boats, training, staff and volunteer stipends required to continue to expand our programs.
