What To Bring

Each participant will need to be sure to have the following items:

  • A well-fitted wetsuit or drysuit.
  • A USCG approved, Type III life jacket.
  • A whistle securely attached to life jacket.

Don't have a life jacket or wetsuit? Please email us or call 800-806-4882 if we can assist you with getting these items together.

Eligibility Criteria

As a matter of safety, to be eligible for our programs each participant must be:

  • Between the ages of 10 and 18.
  • Able to follow instructions and effectively communicate independently (or with the assistance of a companion).
  • Able to independently swim 50 yards (or with the assistance of a companion).
  • Able to independently turn from face-down to face-up and remain floating face-up while wearing a properly fitted life jacket (or with the assistance of a companion).
  • Able to independently get out and from under a capsized paddlecraft (or with the assistance of a companion).

Our programs are open to everyone. Please email us or call 800-806-4882 if you would like to participate but require special accomodations.

Cancellation Policy

We require at least 14 days cancellation notice prior to your scheduled program, or we cannot provide a refund. Our programs require at least 3 participants. If your scheduled program is canceled, we'll notify you at least 14 days prior to the start of the program.

All of our activities are weather dependent, and may be cancelled due to inclement weather or other unsafe conditions. If your scheduled tour or program is cancelled due to safety concerns, you'll receive a full refund.

Scholarships Available

We pride ourselves on making our programs accessible to our community. Scholarships are available to help with the costs of program fees, equipment rentals, snacks, and lunches. Please email us or call 800-806-4882 for more information.

What's Next?

The next step in our kayak program is our Intermediate Kayak Camp, which includes an overnight camping trip. After that, it's on to our Advanced Kayak Camp, which takes place on Yaquina Bay in Newport.
