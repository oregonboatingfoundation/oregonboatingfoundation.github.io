Directors & Advisors

Our volunteer board members and advisors are responsible for strategic planning, governance, and fundraising. They are the backbone of the Oregon Boating Foundation, and we are grateful for their service.

Our board meets monthly, and we are always on the lookout for engaged people to join our board.
