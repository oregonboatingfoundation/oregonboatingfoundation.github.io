Sea Kayak Classes

Book Online

Learn to Sea Kayak

Our one-day sea kayak classes – designed primarily for adults – help build the skills to confidently enjoy kayaking on your own. These affordable classes taught by American Canoe Association certified instructors meet from 9am until 4pm, and we provide the boats and related equipment needed to participate.

Kayak Classes

Our one-day sea kayak classes – designed primarily for adults – help build the skills to confidently enjoy kayaking on your own.

Introduction to Kayaking

Introduction to Kayaking

Get started in sea kayaking with this entry level class by learning basic strokes, maneuvering, rescues, and safety preparation.

Learn More
Essentials of Kayak Touring

Essentials of Kayak Touring

Expand your skills by learning how to safely enjoy paddling on lakes, calm protected ocean environments, and other flatwater destinations.

Learn More
Coastal Kayaking

Coastal Kayaking

Deal with marine hazards and other safety issues by learning additional rescues, the influence of weather and tides, and how to refine your strokes.
